Welcome to Droop

Droop allows you to collaboratively create drawings.
On the Canvas page, you will be greeted with a canvas and a prompt that will tell you what to add to the canvas.
Your canvas may or may not have some art on it already.
Once you are finished adding your contributions to the piece of art, you can submit it.
Afterwards be sure to explore our gallery and see the other pieces of artwork that users on Droop have created.
